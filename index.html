Flood Escape 2 is an parkour game. The goal is to press buttons and reach the end without getting yourself killed by 3 different kinds of water: Water, Acid & Lava. There are different levels: Easy, Normal, Hard, and Insane. Some of the maps you need to be very carful and fast, otherwise, you will drown and die. If you are in water, don't worry because you'll have air, but if you used all the air in your air tank, you'll die too. Try to come up on the surface on time, otherwise, the results won't be good! Come play and try it out, can you handle the challenge?
Tips: Try to make as much shortcuts as possible, it'll save you a lot of time! Be fast, but don't rush!

Icebreaker is a game that you use a breaker to hit people. If you hit someone with your breaker, they'll freeze, and if you got hit by other's breakers you'll get freezed. The way you can continue playing is that people in your team need to hit you again or to thaw you. There will be two teams, the goal for each team is that they need to freeze all the people on the other team. Winners get at least 50 coins! Every 2000 coins, you can choose to open a chest. Chests are for you to have get new stuff! Items like: faces, breakers, trails, etc. It's possible to get a item that you already had, therefore, you'll earn additional coins!
Tips: To freeze people easily, you need to jump and left click at the same time! Try to avoid people from the other team and thaw your teammate!
